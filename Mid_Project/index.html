Common Issues & Quick Fixes

Author: Help Desk Team
Date: November 18, 2025
Category: Troubleshooting Guide
content:

"Most common issues we solve every day - here are quick fixes for you! Issue 1: Can't Login to Portal?

    Quick Fix:
  • Click the "Reset Password" button
  • Check your email (including spam folder)
  • Follow the reset link to create new password
  • If still not working, call Help Desk (Ext. 205)
  • Resolution time: Usually 5 minutes
Issue 2: Attendance Record Wrong?
    Quick Fix:
  • Visit Academic Office, Room 301
  • Provide your Roll Number
  • Get teacher's approval
  • Submit corrected form
  • Update within: 2 working days
Issue 3: No Hostel Room Assigned Yet?
    Quick Fix:
  • Check online allotment status
  • If "Pending" - wait 2 more days
  • If delayed, contact Hostel Office (Ext. 204)
  • Emergency: Temporary room available
  • Resolution: Usually 1-2 days
Issue 4: Need Certificate or Notification?
    Quick Fix:
  • Check if available on portal (usually free)
  • If not available, request at Help Desk
  • Processing fee: 500 PKR if urgent
  • Delivery time: 3-5 working days
  • Standard service: Free
Issue 5: Any Other Problem?
    Quick Fix:
  • WhatsApp: +92-300-XXXX-XXX (Instant)
  • Call: 0800-HELP-UOL (24/7 Toll-free)
  • Visit: Help Desk Counter, Room 201
  • Online: Submit a ticket here on this portal
Don't worry - we handle it all!"
